Winning Team:

PlutoHacks 2023

"PlutoHacks", previously known as "BC Hackathon", is the name of Broward College's annual hackathon, which has been held every year since 2021 under the PlutoHacks name. In 2023, I participated in the event with a team of three other students to work on a project for that year's theme of "Design Our Future".

Together, our team worked on a prototype for an artificial intelligence powered, VR-based job interview simulation environment. The program was built using Unity's game engine, and further utilized OpenAI's ChatGpt API to generate relevant job interview questions (based on a user's initial form input) for any job which the user would like to practice applying for.

It was the first time any of us had participated in a Hackathon, and out of 105 participants, we won 2023's award for Best First Time Hack.
